03 — Approach

Why Athari

Athari comes from the Arabic أثر — a trace, a mark, a signal. That's the standard: every issue should leave one.

  1. Signals, not noise

    Practical intelligence you can act on — no recycled press releases, no engagement bait, no fifty-link dumps.

  2. Regional focus

    Built for the Middle East's market, not adapted to it. The context, constraints, and opportunities here are different — we write from inside them.

  3. AI-aware hiring

    We track how AI reshapes engineering roles and hiring itself — which skills compound, which fade, and what that means for teams and careers.

  4. Engineering-first insight

    Written by people who understand the work. Deep enough for senior engineers, clear enough for the boardroom.

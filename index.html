04 — Approach

Why Athari

Athari comes from the Arabic أثر — a trace, a mark, a signal. That's the standard: every issue should leave one.

  1. Signals, not noise

    Every item earns its place: what happened, why it matters here, what to do about it. No fifty-link dumps, no recycled press releases.

  2. Written from inside the region

    GCC context first — regulation, sovereign cloud, government programmes, and the market's real constraints — not a global feed with "Middle East" in the subject line.

  3. Engineering-first, market-aware

    Deep enough for senior engineers, clear enough for the boardroom — with the hiring market covered as seriously as the technology.
